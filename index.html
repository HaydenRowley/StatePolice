Tactical Response Team

A team of specially trained officers who work with high powered weapons. This team is responsible for responding to critical situations.

Traffic Enforcement Unit

This unit mainly focuses on keeping motorist and pedestrians safe on the roads and streets.

Criminal Investigation Unit

A team of detectives that investigate multiple aspects and situations.

Special Vehicle Unit

This unit is made up of people who work respond to situations that most officers wont go to such as air and marine.

Information

The San Andreas State Police, simular to the name, has jurisdiction to all highways in the state of San Andreas.

Weekly Meetings

Family and professionalism is always at the heart of our division. We also like to praise people for doing things within patrols.

Because of what we believe, we ensure to have weekly meetings that allow us to connect and communicate with members of our department. These meetings include information on things happening to the department, and promotions for people who have done good in the week.
